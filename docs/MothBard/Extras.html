Cool Bug facts:

Laetuli government, society and isolationism is based upon the (now long gone) medieval country of Dithmarschen and the conduct of pirate republics of the 16th century.

The whole “Songs of the Avant Garde” bit is heavily inspired by the Jester from Darkest Dungeon, and so is part of the biography, the images that precede the titles of the sections are also from Darkest Dungeon being that they are skill icons. (If you couldn’t tell i really like that game)

Realistically Maple’s would be unable to even pronounce their own name, seeing as without lips one cannot pronounce M’s, P’s or B’s. But let's just ignore that okay?
