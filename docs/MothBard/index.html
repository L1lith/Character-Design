Certificate of Citizenship

The most serene kingdom of

~Simul~

The following document certifies that; Maple’s Bloom has been granted by his majesty -and the powers invested within him by the gods themselves- the status of an official citizen of Simul and Subject of the one and the only Jeffrey Fortesque first of his name, any and all mandates and dictates originated from the great crown of Simul are to henceforth be enforced upon said citizen, it is now their duty to pay tribute to the crownlands and remain forever loyal to the Fortesque heredity.
