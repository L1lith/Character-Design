Lilith

Base Stats

Age: 23

Height: 6’7” (relatively slim despite being tall, though the hood gives a larger appearance, muscles are very toned to minimize width while still being strong)

Weight: 325lb

Species: Metallic Erinye

Gender: Female

Marital Status: Unmarried

Residence: Nomad

Appearance

Hair: Dark Brown base with multicolored sheen (human like hair with a thin coating of iron)

Eyes: Green

Skin: Primarily dark blend of the colors of natural iron

Special Markings or Attributes: Scars from serious wounds don’t regrow iron, leaving patches of bare skin

Normal Dress: Hooded with a large amount of gear

Personality

Generally very relaxed and friendly though she can be a bit of a loner. She can become angry or on occasion violent when she or someone else is treated unjustly, so she’s a bit of a vigilante/rebel.

Abilities

  • Coded

Very analytically intelligent, ability to decipher and recreate any kind of pattern or language. This includes the ability to communicate with animals, and speak in other languages once she’s heard them, or perform skilled predictions and analysis.

  • Iron Skin

The outer layer of Lilith’s skin is made of iron. It is sort of like a biological light armor, or thin iron exoskeleton. It is thin enough not to hinder her movements, however it is still considerably heavy, so she has a lot of muscle to compensate, making her a massive 325lbs.

This weight means that she consumes a fairly large amount of energy, leading her on moral conquests to feed her hunger using her Destiny Apple (this would be actions that cause right in the world, however she sees it).

This skill also lends very heavily to hand to hand combat. With her large body she has high traveling speed, however in a fight her iron skin makes her agility very average so quick opponents can use this to their advantage, however she compensates for this somewhat with her long limbs and armor properties, using her superior reach to keep her opponent from landing vital blows, and dulling the ones that do land, basically a tank. Ranged or flying opponents pose the greatest threat, however her flying companion Jade can compensate for this to some degree.

  • Speed Information

Traveling (Normal Gear): 10 mph

Traveling (Little to no Gear): 14 mph

Max Sprinting (Normal Gear): 25 mph

Max Sprinting (Little to no Gear): 31 mph

She also can be quick to overheat during sustained activity or in hot environments, especially if she is not adequately hydrated. When she overheats her iron skin layer gradually peels off, eventually leaving her body effectively that of a regular (albeit large, bald, and with no fingernails) human if left untreated (this also is not permanent, she can slowly regenerate if she cools down and consumes more iron). Biologically speaking, except for changes related to her skills she is quite similar to a tall human, especially as far as her internal organs and skeletal structure etc, anything non-skin related. Her muscles are medium sized, but very toned, providing a lot of strength.

Jade

(Companion)

This small winged lizard (not a dragon, much smaller and no fire, sits on shoulder generally) loves to bond with Lilith through their adventures. Jade provides Lilith with vital scouting with it’s flight ability and fast speed. It also provides her with emotional support.

Abilities

  • Venomous bite

Induces pain & paralysis for up to a few hours or so depending on the target species, but is generally non-lethal. This move can be risky as it requires getting up close to the target, but payoff is good, and the risk can be mitigated by using sneak attacks. Targets 20 lbs or less are at serious risk of death. Can only be used roughly once a day as it takes time for the poison to be restored.

  • Flight

Jade flies very quickly and agilely through the sky. This is very useful for scouting purposes or landing it’s other abilities like venomous bite on ranged targets.

  • Claws & Tail

Jade has long, sharp claws, and a long whip-like tail. Both are decent options for aerial attacks, however Jade’s attack power is lower than most creatures given it’s small size, so it’s ability to deal damage can be limited. Jade’s battle strategy usually is to go for venomous bite instead, however if she needs to she can swoop in for a quick swipe, and hope to dash back out before the opponent gets a strike back. Also as a reptile, scratches from her can also lead to nasty infections in many species if the wound is not cleaned properly, which can eventually result in amputations or sometimes even death if left to fester.

Items of Note

  • Item: Destiny Apple

The Destiny Apple is a magical apple she can consume, and it provides her with a variety of nutrients (including certain hard to find minerals), whose quantity is based on how much she is at peace with herself. Eating it never consumes the item, however she can still starve if she betrays her sense of self. It is required to nourish her long-term, however she can eat other foods to fulfill other nutritional needs.

  • Weapon: Metal Nails, Hair & Teeth

Not unlike how human’s skin cells die and form into nail & hair after they’ve fulfilled their purpose, so does Lilith’s iron skin ability. At the end of her fingers she has large pointed claws made of iron, which she grinds against rough rocks to sharpen and file. Her hair is also coated in iron, allowing her to use it as a whip if grown sufficiently long (she usually does this, and keeps it hidden inside her hood). Her teeth are also made of metal and can regrow, they are also mostly human shaped except the canines are especially large and sharp.

Miscellaneous Gear

  1. 3x large water canteens
  2. iron supplements (prunes, nuts, iron dust/shavings, etc)
  3. sometimes a rock for shaving down the claws depending on the environment
  4. any currency, though usually not a lot

Additionally Lilith is experienced in wilderness survival, so she can obtain food while traveling that way. This however can lead to her becoming nutritionally deficient, as wandering the wilderness for too long would cause the Destiny Apple to cease providing essential nutrients. She harvests valuable resources in nature (herbs, rare animal parts, rare rocks, etc) and sells them when she visits cities in order to make money.

Backstory

Lilith was created by an experiment by a cult of religiously driven mad scientists. For this reason she deeply hates corrupt social hierarchies. In a painfully stereotypical fashion, she murdered the cult that had created her. She left her empty village and began wandering around aimlessly until she found Simul.

She is comparable to a sort of biological machine hybrid. She’s not a computer, however she shares similar character attributes (she incorporates large quantities of iron into her body, and she is highly intelligent/analytical, but she has more difficulty breaking into her creative mind, though ironically she can be quick to express her more primal emotions). She also has memory issues so she can often be forgetful.

Her will and hunger are physically linked through her Destiny Apple which was created alongside her, and it drives her to seek justice in the world. She’s sort of an outsider because of her iron skin and large size, and she has a strong bond with her lizard. She also can get a lot of hate from people who see her actions as drastic, or claim that she has no right to interfere as an outsider. She travels from city to city, trying to find happiness for herself and others. She is challenged by homelessness, and forgetting her purpose, sometimes leading her to act out of hunger for better or worse.
